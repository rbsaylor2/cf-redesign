About Us Why we do what we do

Providing quality safety footwear with exceptional service and value is WHAT we do. To really know ABOUT US you need to know WHY we do it.

And it's simple - we do it for you. Our heritage shows that we've always done it for the hard-working companies and their dedicated employees who need a quality pair of safety footwear they can trust, as well as a service provider they can rely on. We value the relationships we've built and continue to build in order to protect the soles of those we serve. It's never been about our bottom line - it's always been about towing the line to ensure your safety and peace of mind.

Our Mission Statement

Building new customer partnerships while strengthening our current valued relationships on the foundation of a leaner CustomFit safety footwear program.
